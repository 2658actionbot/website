FIRST Inspires

Founded by Dean Kamen in 1989, FIRST, “For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology,” is an organization designed to help spread the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM). Its mission is to “inspire young people to be science and technology leaders by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering and technology skills" . With over 9000 teams competing worldwide, FIRST hosts annual robotics competitions. For more information, visit firstinspires.org.
