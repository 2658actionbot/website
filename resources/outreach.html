Community Outreach

Moving Towards a Better Tomorrow

Team Σ-Motion seeks to build a stronger community through community outreach activities and workshops. Our FIRST STEMs program empowers students to learn more about STEM fields by having our team members visit local elementary and middle schools to talk and provide demonstrations of our work. We send team members to the San Diego Festival of Science and Engineering, RB Alive, and the Mini Maker Fair as well as hosting our own programs at the San Diego Public Library to spread STEM. We often visit and support FLL, FTC, and other FRC teams in our area as we believe a future of tomorrow requires everyone's support.
